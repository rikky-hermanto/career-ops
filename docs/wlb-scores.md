## Key Takeaways

**Best WLB bets (≥ 4.0):** Remote.com, Grafana Labs, GitLab, TripAdvisor, Elastic, Xero, PostHog, Supabase — all remote-first or GPTW-certified.

**Fintech pattern:** Almost every fintech in the pipeline scores ≤ 3.4. Regulatory overhead, on-call expectations, and hustle culture are endemic to the sector. Exceptions: Monzo (3.9) and Xero (4.0) stand out.

**AI lab caveat:** Anthropic (3.6) and Perplexity (3.9) are decent, but Cohere (2.6) is a notable outlier — reviews flag unrealistic expectations and poor management. All three have fewer than 50 Glassdoor reviews, so treat scores with some caution.

**Developer tooling premium:** Companies building tools for developers (Grafana, Elastic, GitLab, Supabase, PostHog, Pulumi, WorkOS, Checkly) consistently score higher. Engineering-led cultures tend to value async and output-over-presence norms.
